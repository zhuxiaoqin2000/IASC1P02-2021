A brief introduction to Guangzhou

Guangzhou is located in north of Zhu Jiang Triangles Shoal. It is the politics, economy and culture center of Guangdong Province and strides across both sides of Zhu Jiang River.The area of the city is more than 7,400 square kilometers. The urban district is more than 120 square kilometers and there are more than 3,300,000 people live in the city.Guang Zhou is a city with a long history. It was built in 862 B.C., the year when King Zhou Yi Wang was alive, which was more than 2,800 years ago. The city has other two names: Rams City and Ears of Rice City. Guang Zhou is famous for its traditional food and places of interest. Let's take a look!

Traditional Food


Cantonese cuisine is one of the eigh great traditions of Chinese cuisine. For eating,Guangzhou is the place to be. The food of Guangzhou is distinctive,you can eat different delicious food here. Cantonese enjoy the moment when they have tea and dim sum in the morning, together with their families and friends. Most of the foods are delicate, but the taste is absolutely marvelous.

  • Shrimp dumpling
  • Chicken feet
  • Steamed ribs
  • Ginger Milk Pudding
